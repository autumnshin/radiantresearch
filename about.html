About Us

Grace Pluemacher

Hello! My name is Grace Pluemacher and I am a senior at the Marine Academy of Technology and Environmental Science, and the co-founder of Radiant Research. I wanted to create Radiant to show others the opportunities independent research holds on all levels. While I love hearing about all kinds of different research, my favorite areas are engineering and behavioral science!

William Li

Hi, I'm Will. I’m a senior at Delbarton School where I founded the Research in Science Club. While I enjoy all sorts of science, my research interests are centered around Machine Learning, Differential Equations, and Stochastic Optimization, and I have 4 published papers. My biggest aspiration in science is to discover Bigfoot!

Autumn Shin

Hello! I’m Autumn, a junior at the Bergen County Academies. My main research interests are in math and computer science, and I help lead the math, computer science, and physics teams at my school. Some of my favorite areas to study are game theory, linear algebra, and graph theory.
