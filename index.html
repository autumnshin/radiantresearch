    About Us

    This is Radiant Research.

    Radiant Research is a student-led, New Jersey based research organization. We are an inter-school research network specializing in independent research. Our goal is to matriculate more students into the powerful (and fun) opportunities scientific research holds, including resources, workshops, and guest speakers relating to independent research. We hold monthly research conferences, in which students are given the opportunity to share the research they have been working on to and inspire others.

    Our next conference will be held on January 9th, 2021, so check back for additional information on the research that will be shared and the link to attend! If you are interested in attending or sharing your research, please sign up here!

    We will also hold a research symposium on May 22, 2021, during which students can present and be judged on their research projects.
